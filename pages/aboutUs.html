About Us

Revolutionizing Passenger Navigation

Our cutting-edge Indoor Positioning System (IPS) is designed to transform the passenger experience in railway stations. By combining real-time navigation, advanced technology, and user-friendly interfaces, we make navigating complex station environments seamless and efficient.

With interactive 2D maps, AR navigation, and step-by-step voice guidance, passengers can reach their destinations safely and quickly. Our system ensures accessibility for all users, including voice-based controls for visually impaired passengers and staff assistance alerts for specially-abled travelers. The web portal empowers DRM officers to maintain and update accurate, floor-wise station maps, tested rigorously before deployment. Feedback from users helps us continuously improve, making every journey better.

Implementation Process

How Our Indoor Positioning System Works

STEP 1
Blueprint Upload

Admins upload the station's floorwise blueprints onto our web portal, ensuring an accurate base map for navigation.

STEP 2
Interactive Mapping

Key areas, paths, and nodes are marked on the map to enable dynamic route calculations and seamless navigation.

STEP 3
Testing & Feedback

Selected station staff test the map's accuracy and usability. Their feedback is incorporated into the system.

STEP 4
Deployment & Navigation

The finalized map is deployed for passenger use, providing real-time navigation and additional support via kiosks.
